ACHIEVEMENTS


Global Tech Innovator Recognition:

The School of Computers received international acclaim for innovative contributions to technology and entrepreneurship, securing the first position at the Asiatic Robotics Competition - Robotex Asia 19 in Shanghai, China. The team not only showcased exceptional robotics skills but also earned the prestigious Best Performance Award in the Entrepreneurship category.

Double Triumph at ROBOTEX 2019:

Students from the School of Computers clinched the international championship in the Entrepreneur category at ROBOTEX 2019 in Tallinn, Estonia, Europe. Their groundbreaking ideas also earned them the Best Innovative Idea Award, highlighting their prowess in combining technical excellence with creative innovation.

Chhatra Vishwakarma Award Distinction:

The School of Computers achieved yet another milestone by being selected in the AICTE central and western region for the prestigious "Chhatra Vishwakarma Award." Their outstanding contributions to technology and innovation earned them an invitation to the National Convention held in New Delhi.

Eminent Remote Center Nomination:

In recognition of their commitment to quality education and technological advancements, the School of Computers at IPS Academy has been nominated as a remote center for upcoming Faculty Development Programs (FDPs) under the E & ICT Academy, NIT PATNA. This nomination is a testament to the school's leadership in advancing education and research in the field of computer science.

International Collaboration Initiatives:

Students and faculty from the School of Computers actively engaged in fostering international collaborations, establishing the school as a global player in technology and innovation. These collaborations aim to promote knowledge exchange and provide students with diverse learning opportunities.

Research Excellence Showcase:

The School of Computers has consistently demonstrated a commitment to cutting-edge research, contributing to advancements in various domains of computer science. Their research output has been recognized both nationally and internationally, reinforcing the school's position as a hub for research and innovation.
