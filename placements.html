PLACEMENTS




Diverse Industry Footprint:

Premier companies actively recruit from various sectors, including BFSI, IT/ITES, Digital, E-commerce, FMCG, Product, Automotive, Engineering, ensuring a wide array of career opportunities for students.

Corporate Giants at the Doorstep:

Renowned establishments like Deloitte, TCS, Intel, IBM, Wipro, Infosys consistently extend numerous job offers, recognizing the caliber of students from the School of Computer.

Abundant Placement Offers:

The School of Computer witnesses a multitude of placement offers, signifying the high demand for its graduates in the ever-evolving job market.

Internship Excellence:

Over 70% of students actively participate in internships annually, gaining hands-on experience and practical insights that enrich their academic learning.

Strategic Training Initiatives:

Tailored training programs focus on Placement Orientations, providing students with a competitive edge in the job market. The emphasis on future scope ensures that students are well-equipped with the latest industry trends and skill sets.

Striking a Balance:

The school's approach to placements goes beyond securing jobs; it's about crafting fulfilling careers that align with students' aspirations and potential.
